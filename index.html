Introduction

A short and simple introduction about me (Jules).

Hello! I'm Jules, I was born in the Philippines at December 1, 2004. Just like many teenagers, I enjoyed playing games. I specifically enjoy playing the game called Minecraft. Although Minecraft is a very fun game to build, fight, explore, and lots of stuff to play around with. I was the engineer type of player, I was fascinated by how you can make machines using redstone dusts as wiring and even mess with the own game by running commands using command blocks. It became my specialty when playing Minecraft, helping my friends make machines to make tasks simpler and efficient.

After some time, I discovered Programming and was happy how it was almost correlated to the things I like doing in Minecraft. I tried watching beginner-friendly programming youtube tutorial videos, made my first "Hello, World" program using Python and immediately fell in love with the Art Of Programming. Months passed by, I discovered Computer Science and went on and researched about it. After reading about it, I learned that it teaches people about algorithms, data structures, and many more all of which are really useful when making complicated programs that does complex tasks. I searched for Computer Science courses online and later learned about Harvard's CS50 and immediately enrolled, In hopes of extending my knowledge further on technology, and programming.

Fun Fact: I found this pretty cool Minecraft bot when I was learning Programming. The name of the bot is Baritone, it supports pathfinding, it builds for you, and many more features yet to come. This is where I started seeing Programming as a complex yet beautiful process, and later lead me to discover Computer Science.
